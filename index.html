WELCOME TO A PEACEFUL PLACE

There are various Virtual Reality simulations to make a negative mindset a positive one.

S U M M A R Y

I created simulations via Virtual Reality for different negative mental states including: stress, anxiousness, overwhelmed, and despondency. The simulations were created with the purpose of reducing and reversing the effects of those harmful states. Each simulation is around 5 to 6 minutes, has a serene and beautiful surrounding, features a calming guided meditation or excerise, and provides extensive research information.

STRESS

My Definiton: Stress is defined as a state of mind that leads to negative thinking and frustration over specific present circumstance(s).

ANXIOUSNESS

My Definiton: Anxiousness is defined as a state of mind negatively focused on unknown outcomes of specific events.

OVERWHELMED

My Definiton: Overwhelmed is defined as a state of mind with the perception that certain task(s) or combination of tasks are emotionally consuming.

DESPONDENCY

My Definiton: Despondency is defined as a state of mind of unhappiness, hopelessness, and non-motivation.
