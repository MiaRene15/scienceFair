Deep Breathing for Overwhelmed

Length: 6:02 (minutes:seconds)

Reasoning: People often become overwhelmed by attempting to resolve or focus on too many things at once. Deep breathing exercises hones the mind to focus on one thing: your breath. I believe a deep breathing guided exercise will bring individuals feeling overwhelmed to a more positive mindset.

Background Research: Deep breathing exercises are a part of the practice of Mindfulness. Mindfulness is the practice of focusing on one thing at a time like the feel of silk, the texture and taste of a raisin, etc. Studies have shown that practicing mindfulness increases productivity, increases immune response, decreases stress and the feeling of overwhelmed, and lowers cortisol levels and blood pressure.
