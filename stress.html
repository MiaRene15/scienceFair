Confidence for Stress

Length: 6:31 (minutes:seconds)

Reasoning: Individuals with less stress have more confidence in themselves. I believe a guided meditation about confidence will bring individuals experiencing stress to a more positive mindset.

Background Research: Emmy E. Werner, a research psychologist at University of California, Berkeley, studied the lives of 700 children with acholic parents over 40 years. She wanted to determine the qualities of people who overcome stressful situations. One of those qualities was a strong belief in themselves to resolve problems aka confidence.

Directions: You may stand or sit in the chair for this simulation. If you don't already have VR Goggles on, please click start and put them on right away.
